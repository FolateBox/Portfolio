Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper
  • 15oz of ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Add onion and garlic, cooking until soft.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg until well combined.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a 9x13-inch baking dish: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of noodles, then half of the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella, and repeat the layers.
  7. Top with remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips

  • Make ahead: Lasagna can be prepared a day in advance and baked when needed.
  • Add veggies: Consider adding spinach or mushrooms for extra flavor and nutrition.
  • Use fresh herbs for a more vibrant taste.
