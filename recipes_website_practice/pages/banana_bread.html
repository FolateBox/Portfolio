Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 & 1/2 cup all purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. Mash the ripe bananas with a fork in a mixing bowl. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the flour and stir until just incorporated. Be careful not to over-mix.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool in the pan for a few minutes, then remove the banana bread from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack.
  8. Slice and serve the banana bread warm or at room temperature.

Tips

  • Use very ripe bananas: The riper the bananas, the more flavorful your bread will be.
  • Add-ins: Consider adding a handful of chocolate chips or nuts for extra texture and flavor.
  • Storage: Store banana bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or freeze for longer storage.
